phD 2021 - 2024
Firstly, I am enhancing the mitoXplorer 2.0 by incorporating a single-cell analysis section thereby creating version 3.0.
Additionally, I am developing a sister platform called ataxiaXplorer, building its unique interactome by gathering RNA-seq data from various spinocerebellar ataxias and conducting analyses to identify shared genes.
The ultimate aim is to provide users with a tool to explore their data more comprehensively within the contexts of both ataxia and mitochondria.

Teaching 2020 - 2024
I have been teaching R and mySQL in Master 1, Licence 2 & 3 writing, doing and correcting courses, trainings and exams.

Bioinformatics engineer
IBDM provided me with the opportunity to pursue my Master 2 internship, which resulted in the publication of my RNA-seq analysis R-shiny App called RNfuzzyApp. During this year, I analyzed RNA-seq, ATAC-seq, DamID and CUT&RUN data and contributed to mitoXplorer 2.0 and Annominer .

Master interships
M1 - Characterizing molecular trajectories to elucidate the mechanisms underlying tumor cell interactions in various conditions. To achieve this, I conducted a Mean Squared Displacement (MSD) adjustment.
Arnaud Sergé, "Leuko/Stromal Interactions in Normal and Pathological Hematopoiesis" team at the CRCM. Report (in french)

M2 - Bulk RNA-seq data analysis in D.melanogaster.
Ultimately, I developed an R-Shiny app to empower biologists to conduct their analyses independently. This experience underscored the significance of creating a user-friendly interface.
Bianca Habermann Computational Biology team at the IBDM. Report (in english)
